Welcome to MovieMaker

MovieMaker allows you to create a stop-frame animations directly in your browser. Create a movie and download it, then share to YouTube, Facebook, Google Drive, etc. Sign up with a simple username and password to get started (no email address required)

No Install

MovieMaker uses your webcam directly in the browser (Chrome recommended). You do not need to download and install any software provided you have a working webcam (eg. if you use video in Skype, MovieMaker should be able to use your webcam)

Easy To Use

MovieMaker is really simple. Just click the camera icon to add frames to your movie. Delete frames, preview your results, adjust the movie's speed, and use "onion skinning" to accurately position your model between frames (this shows the previous frame underneath the live webcam preview

Instant Results

Finished your masterpiece? When you're happy with your movie simply click the download button to save it to your hard disk in MOV format. Play in most video players or share to YouTube, Facebook, etc. Movies expire after 24 hours so be sure to save your movie!
